Who am I?

Hello! I'm Alex and I'm a mechanical engineering student at UCLA. Out of high school, I chose to go to my local community college, El Camino College, before transferring to a university. I spent two years there and in the Fall of 2024 transferred to UCLA where I'm currently pursuing my B.S. in mechanical engineering.
